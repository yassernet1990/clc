Capabilities

Full-scope MEP, engineered around performance.

Fire & life safety

Firefighting systems

Complete sprinkler, standpipe, hydrant, suppression and alarm interfaces with hydraulic coordination, testing and authority-ready documentation.

  • Automatic sprinklers & standpipes
  • Hydrants, hose reels & special suppression
  • Testing, flushing & certification support

Pumping infrastructure

Fire pumps & pump rooms

Packaged electric, diesel and jockey pump systems coordinated from foundations and headers through controls, ventilation, drainage and acceptance testing.

  • Pump selection & hydraulic verification
  • Room coordination & installation
  • Start-up, flow testing & handover

Comfort & air quality

HVAC & ventilation

Energy-conscious cooling and ventilation systems shaped around occupancy, climate, maintainability and controls integration.

  • Chilled water, DX & VRF systems
  • Ductwork, ventilation & smoke control
  • TAB, controls integration & seasonal testing

Reliable power

Electrical systems

Coordinated power, lighting, emergency and low-current installations with disciplined testing and traceable records.

  • LV distribution, containment & earthing
  • Lighting, emergency power & generators
  • ELV interfaces, testing & energization

Water & public health

Plumbing & drainage

Domestic water, drainage and specialist piping delivered with pressure testing, hygiene controls and practical access for maintenance.

  • Water supply, drainage & stormwater
  • Pumps, tanks & treatment interfaces
  • Testing, disinfection & commissioning
